The Sojourn Tiny House

Welcome to "the Sojourn"

Explore this unique, rustic, yet somewhat scenic getaway in North, MS. The small, quaint space offers one queen size bed in the main bedroom and one sofa-bed (twin size) in the living room and kitchen area.

The Sojourn is an Amish built tiny house/cabin in rural North Mississippi, about a 17 minute ride away from the Oxford Square.

It is unique in that it offers the opportunity to disconnect for better mental health while you travel: you will find no iOT, internet, nor TVs here, encouraging you to relax and enjoy a digital "disconnect" and experience life without tech for a while!

The Story of the Birth of Sojourn

Before the world changed due to COVID, I had a dream, to build something cool.

I prayed and asked God for direction, and I believe He gave me, simply "start small." I had been intrigued by the idea of tiny houses, and so that started the journey. Welcome to the "sojourn" the flagship of a dreams coming to fruition. The "sojourn" takes its name from a word used even in Biblical times, meaning short or temporary stay, and welcomes you to do just that, enjoy your time in a somewhat rural (short 15 min commute from the square) yet beautiful part of the North MS, during your travel to Oxford, MS. Its a custom designed, Amish built, 640 sq foot place, that, slightly exceeds the 400sq ft or less requirement to be be techincally labeled a "tiny house", which is why its labeling has found its place in a new niche market: "Texas-sized" tiny housing. Yes, I kinda made that up, but anyway, its new, and different, Lets just say I am trying to pave a road here, instead of following everybody else, I guess, for what its worth... Either way, hope that you would consider a stay with us on your next visit to Oxford!
