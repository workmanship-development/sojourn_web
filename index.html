Welcome to "the Sojourn"

Explore this unique, rustic, yet somewhat scenic getaway in North, MS. The small, quaint space offers one queen size bed in the main bedroom and one sofa-bed (twin size) in the living room and kitchen area.

The Sojourn is an Amish built tiny house/cabin in rural North Mississippi, about a 17 minute ride away from the Oxford Square.

It is unique in that it offers the opportunity to disconnect for better mental health while you travel: you will find no iOT, internet, nor TVs here, encouraging you to relax and enjoy a digital "disconnect" and experience life without tech for a while!
